Releases an initial missile barrage before guarding a location of the player's choice.

The VTOL Jet is an offensive Killstreak that will rain down firepower on a location that the user chooses. It initially uses rockets to clear the area, then hovers above the area to kill any remaining stragglers for a certain amount of time.
